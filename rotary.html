About This Book

This book covers the rotary waltz in fine detail. Frame, footwork, body position, etc. Make you rotary waltz, smooth, flowing, and precise.

This book does not teach beginning rotary waltz, it is aimed at instructors and advanced dancers, who are interested in the fine details of the dance.

About the Author

The author has been teaching ballroom dance for 20 years, at formal Victorian balls, including The Gaskell Ball, and casual evening dances, like Friday Night Waltz. He focuses on social dances, and has taught dances from Zweifacher to Two-Step. He is passionately interested in teaching the best classes possible and believes in steady improvment for teachers, as well as students.
