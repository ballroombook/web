About the Book

This book contains practical, actionable ideas for how to create and teach a dance class, or how to improve your existing classes, and how you can improve as a teacher.

This book does not focus on any one dance, or steps, it is focuses on the art of teaching social, partnered dances. It is applicable to classes in Swing, Tango, Waltz, Salsa, Cha Cha, Bachata, Foxtrot, Polka or Lindy Hop and more!

About the Author

The author has been teaching ballroom dance for 20 years, at formal Victorian balls, including The Gaskell Ball, and casual evening dances. He focuses on social dances, and has taught dances from Zweifacher to Two-Step. He is passionately interested in teaching the best classes possible and believes in steady improvment for teachers, as well as students.
